How Hybrid Electric Water Heaters Work
A hybrid electric water heater uses a compressor to take heat from the surrounding air and transfers it inside the water tank. It works similarly to a refrigerator, but in revserse. Thr "hybrid" component means that the water heater either via the heat pump, or, when the surrounding air is too cold, it can heat the same as a conventional electric water heater. This means your water heating is as efficient as possible, and also ensures you have hot water in all environments. Content 2
